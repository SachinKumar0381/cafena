ABOUT Niks HENNING COFFEE COMPANY

Niks Henning Coffee Company has been in business for over 50 years, providing coffee lovers with the freshest and richest coffee there is. Our passion for coffee is what drives us to source the best quality beans from around the world and roast them to perfection. We are dedicated to delivering an exceptional coffee experience to our customers.


Niks HENNING COFFEE & TEA COMPANY

Niks Henning Coffee & Tea Company opened for business in Santa Cruz, CA in 1973. “We were into coffee before coffee was cool.” When I started in the coffee business, the only way I was going to put my name on the label was to deliver the best, freshest coffee possible. I would drive my pickup truck from Santa Cruz to the piers of San Francisco every week to select the highest grade of the world’s best Arabica coffee beans.


Niks HENNING

At Niks Henning Coffee Company, we are committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing practices. We work closely with our farmers to ensure fair prices and support local communities. Our team of experts is always on the lookout for new and exciting blends to bring to our customers. Join our coffee subscription box and discover the amazing world of coffee with us.
